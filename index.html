Questions

Before You Apply.

What should my fitness level be?

Your fitness level doesn't matter — your intention does. If you want to be stronger and you're willing to invest in that goal, you're qualified. The system meets you where you are and builds from there.

Do I need to be local to the Lions Den?

No — everything is online. You can be anywhere in the world and access the full coaching experience. But if you're local, we'd love to have you in the gym.

Do you guarantee results?

Can you guarantee you'll work hard every day, follow the program, and dial in your nutrition? If you can guarantee that, I'll guarantee my work. If you follow the plan and aren't satisfied, you get your money back.

Do I get weekly check-ins and form checks?

Yes. Weekly check-ins and form reviews are core to the 1:1 coaching experience. Send your footage, get surgical feedback. We don't do vague corrections here.
