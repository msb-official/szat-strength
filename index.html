Questions

Before You Apply.

What should my fitness level be?

Your fitness level doesn't matter — your intention does. If you want to be stronger and you're willing to invest in that goal, you're already qualified. The system meets you where you are and builds from there.

Do I need to be local to the Lions Den gym?

No — everything we do is online. You can be anywhere in the world and access the full coaching experience through the app. But if you happen to be local, we'd love to have you in the gym.

Do you guarantee results?

Can you guarantee that you'll work hard every day, follow the program, and dial in your nutrition? If you can guarantee that, I will guarantee my work. If you follow the plan and are not satisfied with your results, you'll get your money back. It's that simple.

Do I get weekly check-ins and form checks?

Yes. Weekly check-ins and form check reviews are a core part of the 1:1 coaching experience. Send your footage, get surgical feedback. We don't do vague corrections here.
